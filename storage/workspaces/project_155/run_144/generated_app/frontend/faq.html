FAQ

Usage Questions

How do I sign up for WeatherApp?

Visit our website and click on the "Sign Up" button. Follow the prompts to create your account.

What is my API key?

Your API key is found in your account settings under the "API Access" tab.

Can I use WeatherApp on mobile?

Yes, WeatherApp is available for both iOS and Android devices.

Feature FAQs

What weather data does WeatherApp provide?

We offer current conditions, forecasts, radar, and more for over 100,000 locations worldwide.

How accurate is the weather data?

Our data comes from trusted meteorological sources and is updated every 15 minutes.

Can I customize my weather alerts?

Yes, you can set up custom alerts for specific locations and conditions directly within the app.

Troubleshooting Tips

WeatherApp won't open on my device.

Try force quitting the app or rebooting your device. If issues persist, contact support.

No data is being displayed.

Ensure you have a stable internet connection and try again later. Check for updates in the app store.

The app crashes frequently.

Restart your device and clear the app's cache. If problems continue, contact support with error messages.
